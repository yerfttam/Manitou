Cancellations

Cancellation Policy

Cabins & Lodge Rooms:

  • Full refund if cancelled 60+ days before check-in
  • 50% refund if cancelled up to 30 days before check-in
  • Same policy applies to rescheduling

Tent Sites & A-Frames:

  • Full refund if cancelled 14+ days before check-in
  • 50% refund if cancelled 7+ days before check-in

Cancellations cannot be accepted by email or phone message. You must call (360) 374-6295 and receive a cancellation confirmation email.

Pets

Pet Policy

  • $35 per stay, per pet — advance notice required
  • Welcome in Lodge Rooms (excluding Sacagawea), Cottage Rooms, Rustic Cabins, and Tent Sites
  • $50 charge for unannounced pets
  • Not permitted in the Main Lodge Great Room
  • May not be left unattended in room (even if crated) or on property while guests are away
Children

Children & Families

  • Generally unable to accommodate children under 10 (some exceptions in Winter — call for details)
  • No age restriction in the Eagle/Owl Cottage if both units are booked by the same family or group
  • No age restriction in Outback Cabins or Tent Sites
Groups

Group Bookings & Larger Stays

Group bookings are defined as 4 or more rooms on a single night or in sequence. A 50% deposit is required at time of booking, and special cancellation policies apply — call or email for specifics.

  • Cottage Rooms together accommodate up to 6 persons
  • 2-room Cedar Suite in the Main Lodge sleeps up to 5 guests
  • Lily and Lady units (entire upper floor with mezzanine and exterior deck) accommodate up to 5 guests together
  • Seasonally adjusted rates for booking the entire lodge are available

Questions about our policies or special arrangements? We're happy to help.
