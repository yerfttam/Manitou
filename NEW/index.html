Welcome to

Manitou
Lodge

Manitou Lodge offers lodge rooms, cabins, and camping on ten acres of coastal rainforest, just minutes from Rialto Beach and a short drive from Forks, WA. Built of wood and stone, the lodge centers around a great room with a stone fireplace, vaulted ceilings, and simple spaces made for resting. At night, the forest grows quiet, broken only by the river and the calls of owls and coyotes.

Just minutes from the lush trails of Olympic National Park and the wild beaches of the Washington coast, Manitou places you right where ancient forest and ocean are most. Rialto Beach is 3 miles away. The Hoh Rainforest, 40 miles. Explore by day, rest deeply by night.

Come In From
the Rain

Though it feels tucked far from the noise of everyday life, Manitou sits just minutes from the beaches and rainforest trails of Olympic National Park, including the moss-covered paths of the Hoh Rain Forest.

Guests can spend their days exploring the coast and forest, then return to quiet evenings among old-growth trees, whether staying in the lodge, a cabin, or a simple tent site beneath the canopy.
